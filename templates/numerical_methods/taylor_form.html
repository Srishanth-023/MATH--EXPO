Taylor Series Method Solver

Taylor Series Method Formula:

\[ y(x+h) = y(x) + hy'(x) + \frac{h^2}{2!}y''(x) + \frac{h^3}{3!}y'''(x) + \cdots \]

Where derivatives are calculated from the ODE:

\[ \frac{dy}{dx} = f(x, y) \]

Use 'x' for independent variable and 'y' for dependent variable
